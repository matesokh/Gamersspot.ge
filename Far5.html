Players can explore the map freely, completing missions, recruiting allies known as "Guns for Hire," and engaging in intense battles against cult forces. The game also includes Far Cry Arcade, a mode for creating and playing custom maps and challenges. With its dark narrative, memorable antagonist, and freedom of gameplay, Far Cry 5 received praise for its world design and action, though its controversial themes sparked discussion. It remains one of the most popular entries in the Far Cry series, setting the stage for future sequels
